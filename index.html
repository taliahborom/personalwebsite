Internship with Brave Initiatives

This summer I will be working with Brave Initiatives, a nonprofit that was started in Chicago in 2015.The goal of Brave Initiatives is to empower girls to be agents of change in their own communities using desing, coding, and leadership training. Brave currently provides camps in cities throughout the world such as Chicago,IL Indianpolis,IN Santa Fe,NM, Lancester,PN and Jakarta, Indonesia. These camps last a full week and each day girls learn different skill sets such as working with a team to code a website on an issue of their choice in the areas of Environment, Education, Health, and Social Justice. They also get leadership training, learn how to conduct interviews and collect data, give presentations,and reflect on their individual paths, and the future. Brave Initiative sees the importance of having women and WOC in the tech world to solve critical problems as technology continues to grow!
